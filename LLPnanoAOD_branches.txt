dxyBSSignedErr:     uncertainty of signed transverse impact parameter from the beamspot
ip3DBSSigned:       signed 3D impact parameter from the beamspot, using IPTools
ip3DBSSignedErr:    uncertainty of signed 3D impact parameter from the beamspot
displacedID:        displaced muons ID as recommended by the muon POG
muonMatch1:         number of segment/hit matches between the DSA muon and the (standard) muon with the most number of matched hits

muonMatch4idx:      muon index of the (standard) muon with the forth most number of matched hits
muonMatch5:         number of segment/hit matches between the DSA muon and the (standard) muon with the fifth most number of matched hits
muonMatch5idx:      muon index of the (standard) muon with the fifth most number of matched hits

vx:                           vertex positon x
vy:                           vertex positon y
vz:                           vertex positon z
vxy:                          vertex transverse positon
vxySigma:                     uncertainty of vertex transverse positon
dR:                           Delta R between the two muons
idx1:                         muon index of muon 1
idx2:                         muon index of muon 2
isDSAMuon1:                   if muon 1 is from the DSAMuon collection
isDSAMuon2:                   if muon 2 is from the DSAMuon collection
isDGLMuon1:                   if muon 1 is from the DGLMuon collection
isDGLMuon2:                   if muon 2 is from the DGLMuon collection
